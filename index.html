Who is a cooperative (NEHILA / نحيلة)?

The will and experience of beekeeping professionals in the Souss region (33 beekeepers and 6,000 hives) have come together to form a beekeeping cooperative, producing the best and most popular free natural honey, to inspire the greatest trust of traders and partners, and to reap awards for quality and excellence in many professional exhibitions regional and national. (Best Television Award 2011).

Rich in the long experience of beekeepers, and strong in the quality of local honey extracted from the herbs and plants of the mountains and plains of the south and other regions.
